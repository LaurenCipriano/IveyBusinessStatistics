Survey Variables

  1. YEAR: Year for this respondent
  2. ID: Unique ID
  3. WEIGHT: Combined survey weight variables: WTSSCOMP*OVERSAMP

Respondent Variables

  1. VHAPPY: Respondent’s general happiness 1: Very Happy 0: Pretty Happy or Not too happy
  2. COHORT: Respondent’s year of birth
  3. AGE: Age of Respondent
  4. SEX: Respondent’s sex
  5. MentalHealth: How does the respondent describe their own mental health
    1: Excellent 2: Very Good 3: Good 4: Fair 5: Poor
  6. BMI: Body Mass Index (Collected for adults)
  7. HasHypertension: Has the respondent been diagnosed with high blood pressure?
    1: Yes 0: No
  8. HasDiabetes: Has the respondent been diagnosed with diabetes?
    1: Yes 0: No
  9. Drinks5Day: Over the past 12 months, did the respondent have 5 or more alcoholic drinks per day?
    1: Yes 0: No
  10. HealthInsurance: What type of health insurance, if any, does the respondent have?
    1: Any Private 2: Public Only 3: Uninsured
  11. HaveProvider: Respondent has a usual healthcare provider
    1: Yes 0: No
  12. NotAffordHealthCare: In the past 12 months, respondent did not receive or delayed medical care because of cost?
    1: Yes 0: No
  13. FamIncome_Continuous: Family total income from all sources
  14. FamIncome_PercentPoverty: Family income divided by the applicable poverty line based on family size and composition
  15. FamIncome_Categorical: Family income as a percent of the poverty line organized as a categorical variable
    1: Poor/Negative 2: Near Poor 3: Low Income 4: Middle Income 5: High Income
  16. TotalHealthExpenditure: Total health care expenditures ($): all sources (public insurance, private insurance, and out-of-pocket)
