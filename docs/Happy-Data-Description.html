FamIncomeAdj Estimated family income adjusted for inflation. Estimated family income level, combining income brackets from nine different survey categorical definitions (INCOME72, INCOME, INCOME77, INCOME82, INCOME86, INCOME91, INCOME98, INCOME06, INCOME16) and then adjusted for inflation to 2023 real dollars.
